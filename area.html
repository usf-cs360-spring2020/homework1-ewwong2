Encoding

The daily average passenger count in the area chart is encoded by the length of the bar, so longer bars represent higher daily average passenger count.

The color encoding is for the flight type (GEO Summary). The blue is for domestic flights and orange is for international flights.

Note that the y-axis is the daily avergae passenger count by month. This is alleviate the issues of shorter or longer months.

Each plot is by month represents the daily average passenger count for the given month.

Conclusion

From this stacked area chart is easy to multiple things:

1. There has been an upward trend in passengers daily over the 2005 to 2019 time period.

2. The amount of daily passengers each year is cyclical. Perhaps correlated to summer break for students?

3. There are significantly more domestic passengers than international ones.
