Data

The dataset comes from DataSF. It contains data about passenger traffic in different airlines. Info about the dataset from the dataset are as follows:

21.2K Rows 12 Columns Each row is Airline Passengers 2.2 MB CSV file size

Columns

  1. Activity Period: The time in a integer form from 200507 (July 2005) to 201909 (September 2019).
  2. Operating Airline: The airline that flew in String format. The includes airlines like United, SkyWest, American Airline, and etc.
  3. Operating Airline IATA Code: The International Air Transport Association code for the operating airline.
  4. Published Airline: The airline that published the flight.
  5. Published Airline IATA Code: The International Air Transport Association code for the published airline.
  6. GEO Summary: The geo summary is the type of flight (Domestic or International).
  7. GEO Region: The geo region is the location of the flight (US, Canada, Asia, Europe, and etc).
  8. Activity Type Code: Type of activity of the flight (Deplaned, Enplaned, or Thru/Transfer).
  9. Price Category Code: The price category of the flight (Low Fare or Other).
  10. Terminal: Terminal of the flight (Terminal1, Terminal2, International, and etc).
  11. Boarding Area: Boarding area of the flight (A, B, C, D, E, or Other).
  12. Passenger Count: Number of passenger in flight as an integer.
