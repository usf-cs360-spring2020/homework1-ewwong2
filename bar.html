Encoding

The passenger count in the stacked bar chart is encoded by the length of the bar, so longer bars represent higher passenger count.

The color encoding is for the region (GEO Region). The color legend is provided.

Data Wrangling

The data for the area chart was from . The exported CSV from can be downloaded here.
Quite a bit of data wrangling was needed to change the data into a form that is supported by d3.stack.

The data was also sliced to only show the top 30 airlines by total passenger count. This is done in JS for quick adjustment.

Conclusion

From this stacked bar chart it can concluded that:

1. United Airlines dominates other airlines in terms of passenger count.

2. US is a very highly visited region for the top 30 airlines by passenger count.
